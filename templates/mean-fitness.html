Mean Fitness Simulation

(Click HERE to go back to the home page)

Description:

This simulation illustrates the relationship between allele frequency and population mean fitness for a simple one-locus, two-allele model of viability selection. Users may enter in a variety of different fitnesses from 0.0 to 1.0.

Instructions:

  • Set a fitness of A11 (range: 0.0 to 1.0; default: w11 = 0.5).
  • Set a fitness of A12 (range: 0.0 to 1.0; default: w12 = 0.5).
  • Set a fitness of A22 (range: 0.0 to 1.0; default: w22 = 0.5).
  • Push "Start" to begin a simulation.
  • Push "Clear" to clear the accumulated results and start over.

How to intepret the graph:

  • The vertical axis shows the mean fitness corresponding to different allele frequencies on the horizontal axis.
